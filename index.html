Welcome to Our Limbo

Your goal is to escape the legal limbo of many immigrants by surviving the passage of time. To win, you must progressively unlock these milestones: Work Permit, Approved Asylum, Permanent Residency (Green Card), and US Citizenship. Random Milestone Cards: During the game, you will unpredictably face the opportunity to advance your legal status to reach these milestones. Tight Victory: If you manage to survive the cost of the final card (Citizenship), you will automatically win the game on the next turn.
